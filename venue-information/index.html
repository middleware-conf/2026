VENUE INFORMATION

Conference Venue: Universitat Rovira i Virgili - Campus Catalunya

The conference will take place at the Campus Catalunya of the Universitat Rovira i Virgili, located in the heart of the city of Tarragona.

Address: Av. Catalunya, 35, 43002 – Tarragona (Spain).

Campus Catalunya facilities

The campus offers more than 55 classrooms and several highly qualified spaces to host the conference activities, including the Aula Magna and the Board Room. General facilities include:

  • All rooms equipped with necessary technology (screen, computer, connectors).
  • Eduroam and conference WiFi is available.
  • There is a dedicated caterer that can provide all the services needed.
  • The building is fully accessible.

Aula Magna Features

Room characteristics

  • Capacity: 354 people
  • Location: building D1, 1st floor
  • Access ramp
  • Climate control: heating and cooling
  • Surface area: 70m2 (stage), 273 m2 (room)

Technical features

  • 2 video projectors (16:9 HDMI)
  • Touchscreen Laptop - Windows10 and Office21 LTSC
  • Option to connect a laptop (HDMI cabling available)
  • Internet connection & Wireless Network
  • Videoconferencing (Microsoft Teams) with 3 Full HD cameras
  • Sound System with 6 table microphones, 2 wireless handheld microphones and 4 headset microphones
  • Podium with microphone

How to get to Campus Catalunya?

From the Renfe train station (by bus):

  • Line 41 to the "Montoliu" stop. You can check the schedules here.
  • Line 21 to the “Campus Catalunya” stop. You can check the schedules here.

From the AVE station (Camp de Tarragona):

The AVE station is located outside the city. You can take a bus from Camp de Tarragona to the city's main bus station. Please check the bus schedules for this route.

Travelling to Tarragona

By Bus

Tarragona has one main bus station for national and international travel. It provides connections to cities across Spain as well as to several European destinations.

By Plane

The best options to arrive in Tarragona are by flying to Reus, or by flying to Barcelona, whose airport is well-connected with many other European cities, or by flying to Reus, one of Tarragona's main cities.

14 km away from Tarragona.

  • Location: View on Google Maps
  • Take bus (~20 minutes). Schedules available here.

87 km away from Tarragona. Two passenger terminals: terminal 1 (largest) and terminal 2 (three connected sections: T2A, T2B and T2C). Free transport between terminals. Each airline operates in a specific terminal, check here to know the terminal you will be landing. To reach the city:
